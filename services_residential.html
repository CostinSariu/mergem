Cabinetry

Whatever your budget, whatever your style, we're experts in design & finely crafted woodwork. From Kitchen & Bath, Mudroom & Laundry, Desk & Office, Closet & Bedroom to Decorative Built-Ins.

Materials & hardware sourced directly from manufacturers, waste reduction software and our small, efficient team means we can do more for a lower cost than our competition.

1) Establish Functionality and Lifestyle
Consider what features and functionalities you'd like implemented based on your lifestyle and habits. When getting to know you, we can help expand your vision through technical details and breakdowns. Cost/value decisions are much more effective when clients truly understand the logic behind their options.

2) Explore Your Budget Options
Your desired design and features are usually available at various price points. We do everything possible to offer solutions that achieve your dream within your budget.

3) Design Strategy
Once we've assessed the location and taken measurements, we'll start by discussing your ideas and inspirations to formulate an initial concept. As we develop toward a spec, you'll receive design iterations to help visualize and understand your choices.

Whether working complimentarily with your design firm or through our services, we pay close attention to every aspect surrounding your project scope, not just our own duties. What's Included

Personalized Design

As we formulate your concept, our designers will construct detailed, realistic design iterations of your project via photos and walkthrough videos. You'll fully vizualize your ideas with the help of industry leading technology and our talented design team.

Budget Friendly Options

To achieve your dream within your budget, we'll provide you with diversified material and design options. We break down material/labour costs to help you understand and prioritize the details and value that matters most to you.

Always Custom Fitted

Larger factories tend to pre-fabricate standard parts to help cut their operating costs. Their designers may sneak this into your project without you noticing. In contrast, our designs are shaped around your unique project without compromise.
