Getting Started

Where do I start?

When considering your project path, looking at finished work published by industry experts can be helpful in conceptualizing your vision. We'll help direct your inspirations through technical guidance and answering your questions along the way!

How long will a quote take?

At our initial consultation we'll get to know each other and establish the bones of the project: Budget, complexity, timeline and measurements. With a plan established at our first meeting, quotes can be provided in as little as 24 hours depending on the complexity of scope and desired materials. We work hard to provide you accurate pricing as nobody likes unexpected costs.

About Mergem Cabinetry

Where are you located?

Mergem Cabinetry is located at Bay#10 5660, 10st, NE, Calgary, AB, Canada. We're right around DeerFoot mall. We stay busy so office meetings are best done by appointment.

What kind of services does Mergem offer?

Our main offering is specialized custom cabinetry, design and unique carpentry. We are often requested in additional aspects of construction and are happy to help where possible. Our general contracting and total renovation services are available by request!

About Our Process

Initial preparations

1) Establish Functionality and Lifestyle
Consider what features and functionalities you'd like implemented based on your lifestyle and habits. When getting to know you, we can help expand your vision through technical details and breakdowns. Cost/value decisions are much more effective when clients truly understand the logic behind their options.

2) Explore Your Budget Options
Your desired design and features are usually available at various price points. We do everything possible to offer solutions that achieve your dream within your budget.

3) Create a Schedule
Project timelines depend on design complexities, iterations and unique project challenges. To keep work on schedule and your expectations met, we'll plan together and keep you involved in the stages and project progressions. We are generally very flexible to your needs.

Consultation & measurements

Give us a call! We're happy to answer any questions over the phone and if you'd like, we can schedule an initial on-site meeting and measurements.

Designing your project

Once we've assessed the location and taken measurements, we'll start by discussing your ideas and inspirations to formulate an initial concept. As we develop toward a spec, you'll receive design iterations to help visualize and understand your choices.

How long will my order take?

This is completely dependant on project complexity. We are very transparent of our capabilities and lead times. Project delays can happen, but we go above and beyond to stick within our established timeline. Being small and dedicated has meant that we generally operate with more speed and adaptability than larger factory sized firms.

How is manufacturing done?

In house! As a small team, our experts handle everything from raw material inspections to polishing the finished product. Strong project-team involvement and awareness is key to our efficient production and flawless attention to detail.

The installation stage

Understanding the frustrations of on-site construction, we place great importance on making it as convenient, fast and pleasant as possible. We do our absolute best to stay flexible and accommodating to your schedule and on-site needs.

All Finished Up

Post installation inspection

We'll set an appointment to go over the final work with you, combing over the quality and noting anything for repair. This is also when we'll decompress the project and your experience, answering any questions and addressing concerns.

Our lifetime warranty

As a huge thank you for choosing us, we offer a lifetime warranty on our work. If anything is not making you smile ear to ear give us a call and we'll be happy to address it. For you it's a continual promise, for us it's a lesson toward perfection.
