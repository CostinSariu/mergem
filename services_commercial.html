What's Included?

Design Meetings

We start by discussing your ideas and inspirations to formulate an initial concept. As we develop toward a spec, you'll receive detailed design iterations to help visualize and understand your choices.

Budget Friendly Options

Your desired design and features are usually available at various price points. To achieve your dream within your budget, we make use of an extensive direct to manufacturer supply network and break down cost/value options.

Always Custom Fitted

Larger factories tend to pre-fabricate standard parts to help cut their operating costs. Their designers may sneak this into your project without you noticing. In contrast, our designs are shaped around your unique project without compromise.

Delivery & Installation

Understanding the frustration of on-site construction, we place great importance on making it as convenient, fast and pleasant as possible. As a small specialized team, we are readily flexibile and accommodating to your schedule and on-site needs.

Lifetime Warranty

As a huge thank you for choosing us, we offer a lifetime warranty on our work. If anything is not making you smile ear to ear give us a call and we'll be happy to address it. For you it's a continual promise, for us it's a lesson toward perfection.

    Solid Wood Species

    Solid wood counters have near endless possibilities and patterns. Common wood types include maple, cherry, oak, birch and teak. Though extremely beautiful with unique finishing options, solid wood counters are significantly less durable and require higher maintenance than natural stone.

  • Category: Countertops

    • Laminate

    Laminate counters are a thin layer usually pressed onto a particle board wooden base. As an inexpensive and low maintenance option, they come in endless styles that mimic expensive surfaces such as granite or marble. Laminate countertops are not resistant to heat or scratches.

  • Category: Countertops

    • Corian

    Corian is a solid surface countertop variant with better quality than laminate though incomparable to the durability and aesthetics of natural stone.

  • Category: Countertops

    • Granite

    The most popular high-end option without a doubt, granite is a natural stone slab that is praised for the unique aesthetics of each piece, high durability and great resale value.

  • Category: Countertops

    • Quartz

    Quartz counters are engineered by combining natural stone with modern manufacturing. The result is a surface directly comparable in beauty to granite while offering greater durability at lower cost.
